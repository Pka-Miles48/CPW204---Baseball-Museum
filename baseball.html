About the Museum

Discover the rich history of baseball through our extensive collection of memorabilia and exhibits. Founded in 2024, Dawstin's Baseball Museum aims to preserve and celebrate the legacy of America's favorite pastime.

Our mission is to educate and inspire visitors by showcasing the evolution of baseball, from its early beginnings to the modern era. We strive to create an engaging and interactive experience for fans of all ages.

The museum features a wide array of exhibits, including rare artifacts, vintage uniforms, and historic photographs. Our collection highlights the achievements of legendary players, iconic teams, and unforgettable moments that have shaped the sport.

In addition to our permanent exhibits, we host special exhibitions and events throughout the year. These rotating displays offer fresh perspectives on the game and provide opportunities for visitors to learn about different aspects of baseball history.

We are committed to community engagement and offer various educational programs, workshops, and outreach initiatives. Our goal is to foster a deeper appreciation for baseball and its cultural significance.

Whether you're a lifelong fan or new to the sport, Dawstin's Baseball Museum has something for everyone. Come and explore the rich heritage of baseball with us!
